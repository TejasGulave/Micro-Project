PCET Student Information System

Organized Academic & Personal Student Records

The PCET Student Information System is a comprehensive web-based project developed to maintain, organize, and display academic and personal information of students in an efficient and accessible manner. The system provides a central platform where both students and faculty members can access important details related to education, courses, and personal records. This project aims to simplify the management of student data, ensuring that information is accurate, up-to-date, and easy to retrieve whenever necessary. By digitizing the academic and personal records, the college can reduce paperwork, minimize errors, and streamline administrative processes. Each student record includes information such as full name, email, phone number, branch of study, academic history, and personal address. The system also allows for storing branch-specific details like curriculum, faculty in charge, lab facilities, and department activities. Faculty members can monitor student progress, attendance, and performance in a structured format, making evaluation more transparent. The design incorporates modern UI/UX principles with responsive layouts, ensuring compatibility with desktops, tablets, and mobile devices. The student registration form is integrated into the website, allowing new admissions to enter their details securely online. With an organized gallery and branch-specific sections, the website also highlights the college’s infrastructure, labs, library, sports grounds, and extracurricular facilities. The automatic image slider on the home page presents a visual introduction to the campus, creating an engaging first impression for visitors.

About PCET

Pimpri Chinchwad Education Trust (PCET) is a well known educational organization located in Pune, Maharashtra. The institution provides quality technical education and focuses on the academic and professional development of students.

The college provides modern laboratories, experienced faculty, a large library, sports facilities, and various technical and cultural activities. The goal is to develop skilled engineers and professionals.

This website project is designed to maintain and display basic academic and personal information of students in an organized and easy to understand format.
