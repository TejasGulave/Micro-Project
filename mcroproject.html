About PCET

Pimpri Chinchwad Education Trust (PCET) is a well-known educational organization located in Pune, Maharashtra. The institution provides quality technical education and focuses on the academic and professional development of students.

The college provides modern laboratories, experienced faculty, a large library, sports facilities, and various technical and cultural activities. The goal is to develop skilled engineers and professionals.

This website project maintains and displays basic academic and personal information of students in an organized, easy-to-understand format.

Information Technology

IT students learn programming, networking, database management, software development, and cybersecurity. PCET provides modern labs, computer facilities, expert faculty, and scholarships for meritorious students.

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical students focus on machines, thermodynamics, manufacturing, and production systems. PCET provides workshops, labs, expert faculty, hands-on learning, and scholarships.

Civil Engineering

Civil students learn construction, design, surveying, and infrastructure development. PCET provides labs, library, project guidance, sports facilities, and scholarships.

Sports & Extracurriculars

PCET encourages students to participate in various sports and extracurricular activities. Facilities include cricket, football, basketball, athletics, and indoor games. Scholarships are provided for sports achievements.

Computer Science

Computer Science students focus on algorithms, programming, AI, and software development. PCET provides labs, internet, workshops, project support, and scholarships.
